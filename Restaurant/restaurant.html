Anthony's Top Five Restaurants in ATX

Austin, TX, has many delicious and incredible restuarants to offer its hungry guests.
From pizza, to sushi, to BBQ that made restuarants famous, Austin has a myraid of choices for every appetite.
Below are a list of Anthony's Top Five Favorite Restuarants in Austin, TX.


Number Five: P. Terry's Burger Stand

Great spot for a quick, affordable burger. There's lots of burger joints in Austin, but P. Terry's takes the cake for delicious and satisfying.
Stop by their location on Barton Springs and Lamar.


Number Four: Ichiban

Are you a fan of sushi? Try out this delicious and affordable Japanese and Korean kitchen. Their specialty rolls are different from what you typically see and are very tasty.
Stop by their location on Burnet and Greenlawn.


Number Three: Peached Tortilla

This once-a-food-truck restaurant now has a brick and mortar location showcasing a collection of Asian fusion dishes. In addition to the great food, try out one of their amazing cocktails.
Stop by their location on Burnet and Shoalmont.


Number Two: Uchiko

Time to step up your dining experience (and give your wallet a workout) with this sushi lover's paradise. Uchiko creates dishes that truly make you reconsider what life means.
Stop by their location on Lamar and 43rd.


Number One: Salty Sow

If you're hunting for delicious, indulgent food coupled with a relaxed homey atmosphere, look no further. Salty Sow takes comfort food and cocktails to a whole new level.
Stop by their location on Manor and Poquito.
